Our Story

Niyantri Cafe began as a simple dream: to create a cozy place where neighbors could gather over good coffee and homemade pastries, using locally sourced ingredients. Our rustic decor celebrates handmade craft and sustainable practices.

From day one, our goal has been to nurture community. We partner with regional farmers, bakers, and artists to bring you seasonally inspired menus and supportive spaces for local creators to collaborate and connect.

“Niyantri Cafe feels like a warm hug — the perfect place to slow down and savor a cup of coffee.”
